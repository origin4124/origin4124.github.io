Episode note

One of the things I keep coming back to as this support path takes shape is that it does not really need to stand entirely alone forever.

A workbook can absolutely be useful on its own. A reflection path can absolutely help a dancer leave class with something clearer and less heavy. But when something like that starts to work, it also starts to point toward something bigger.

Not bigger in the loud sense. Not bigger in the sense of more pressure. And not bigger in the sense of rushing toward the next offer too early.

Bigger in the sense of steadier. More guided. More supportive over time.

That broader path is still being shaped. And I do not want to rush it. Because the priority right now is not escalation. The priority is usefulness. If what exists becomes genuinely useful first, that becomes a much better foundation for anything that grows from it later.
