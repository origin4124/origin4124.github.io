About

Meet the educator behind Thriving Dancer.

Thriving Dancer was created by Trista Kite to give serious young dancers and their families clearer guidance, deeper context, and more whole-person support than class time alone can usually provide.

Back to Start Here Back to Homepage

What makes this approach different

Thriving Dancer is built around the idea that excellent training should be rigorous, intelligent, and humane. Young dancers need more than corrections — they need context, language, and guidance that helps them understand what they are doing and why it matters.

Core credibility to feature

  • BFA in Dance
  • ABT National Training Curriculum Certified Teacher®
  • Professional Dancers’ Graduate Teaching Diploma (Royal Academy of Dance)
  • Certified Pilates Instructor (PhysicalMind Institute)
  • Perfect Pointe System® Certified Teacher
